        <h3>Invoicing &amp; quotes</h3>
        <p>Branded estimates and tax invoices, recurring billing, late-fee automation, customer portal payments. Inbuilt SAE Engineering quote terms.</p>
      </div>

      <div class="feature">
        <h3>Bills &amp; expenses</h3>
        <p>Vendor bills with PO matching, mobile receipt capture, supplier statement reconciliation.</p>
      </div>

      <div class="feature">
        <h3>Bank feeds (real, not scraped)</h3>
        <p>Direct CDR feeds via SISS Data Services. ANZ, CBA, NAB, Westpac, Macquarie and most second-tier banks.</p>
      </div>

      <div class="feature">
        <h3>BAS &amp; GST</h3>
        <p>Quarterly &amp; monthly BAS prep, GST adjustments, ATO SBR e-lodgement via your own RAM Machine Credential.</p>
      </div>

      <div class="feature">
        <h3>STP Phase 2 payroll</h3>
        <p>Single Touch Payroll Phase 2 with the right reporting categories, EOFY finalisation, payment summaries.</p>
      </div>

      <div class="feature">
        <h3>Fixed assets</h3>
        <p>Asset register, prime-cost &amp; diminishing-value depreciation, low-value pool, immediate write-off thresholds.</p>
      </div>

      <div class="feature">
        <h3>Multi-entity, multi-currency</h3>
        <p>Group consolidation, inter-entity loans, FX revaluation. Designed for trust + company group structures.</p>
      </div>

      <div class="feature">
        <h3>Audit trail you can show the ATO</h3>
        <p>Immutable journal log, before/after diffs on every edit, signed export bundles for review.</p>
      </div>

      <div class="feature">
        <h3>API + webhooks</h3>
        <p>Everything the UI does, the REST API does. OAuth2, scoped tokens, per-tenant rate limits.</p>
